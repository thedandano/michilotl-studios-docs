# LockIn Support

LockIn is a group accountability app for structured self-discipline challenges.

For app issues, purchase questions, account or data requests, general feedback, or feature requests, contact:

[dansedano.dev@gmail.com](mailto:dansedano.dev@gmail.com)

When reporting an issue, include:

- Device model and OS version (iOS or Android).
- App version and build number.
- Whether the issue happened during onboarding, creating or joining a crew, daily check-in, the live feed, purchase, or restore.
- What you expected to happen, and what happened instead.

To request access to, export of, or deletion of your account data, email the address above from the account you signed up with.
