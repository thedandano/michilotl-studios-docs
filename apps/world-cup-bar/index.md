# World Cup Bar

World Cup Bar is a macOS menu bar app that shows live 2026 FIFA World Cup scores, upcoming fixtures, and followed-team highlights — directly from your menu bar.

## What it does

- Shows the live score of a match involving a country you follow, or the next upcoming fixture.
- Updates automatically every 30 seconds during live matches, every 5 minutes otherwise.
- Lets you pick the countries you want to follow from the full 2026 tournament team list.
- Displays team abbreviations or flag emoji in the compact menu bar label — your choice.
- Shows a post-tournament placeholder once the 2026 tournament is complete.

## What it doesn't do

- It does not store or share any personally identifiable information.
- It does not require an account or sign-in.
- It does not display ads.

## Data source

Live match data is fetched from the public [worldcup26.ir](https://worldcup26.ir) API. World Cup Bar displays this data as-is and is not affiliated with FIFA or the official tournament organizers.

## Season scope

World Cup Bar covers the 2026 FIFA World Cup. It is sold as a one-time season purchase and is not guaranteed to be updated for future tournaments.

## Version

Current release targets macOS 13 or later.
